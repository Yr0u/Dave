Hey there, I'm David

The dream of moving to Japan lingered like an unsettled account in my heart until I finally decided to make the change later in life, after establishing my life and career in America.

I felt reluctant to give up the lifestyle I had come to enjoy as a freelancer but knew that destiny awaited me across the sea. It took some experimentation but I managed to find a way to immigrate to Japan as a self-employed person. It turned out to be the best decision of my life.

I created the Open World Help Center as a resource for others like me, with hopes that I can help others realize their dream of living in Japan.
